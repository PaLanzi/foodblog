tarte flambée

Tarte flambée, as the French call it, is proof that good recipes don't need tons of ingredients. But good ones! We always put tarte flambée on the table when we don't really feel like cooking. It can be done so quickly that it does not require a long walking time or laborious preparation of other ingredients.

for the tarte flambée we need: 220 g of flour, 2 tablespoons oil, 120 ml of water, 1 pinch of salt, 2 cups of creme fraiche, 1 onion cut into in strips 150 g bacon diced, salt, pepper, nutmeg

Ingredients Quantity Preparation
Flour 220g Put flour in a bowl.
Oil & Water 2 tablespoon, 120ml add Oil and Water in the Bowl with the spices and knead into a smooth dough. and roll it out very thinly.
creme fraiche 2 Heat the oven to 250°C top/bottom and prepare the two cups of Creme fraiche
bacon & onion, salt, pepper, nutmeg 150g, 1 large spices a breeze slice the Bacon and Onion in thin stripes. Now take the creme fraiche and put it on the dough. flawor it with some salt & pepper put the bacon and onion in a nice way on the creme fraiche and put it in the oven for about 5-8 mins.
White Wine How much you can handle Enjoy the tarte flambée with a glass or bottle of wine ;)
